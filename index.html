About Moroccan Law Assistant

This AI-powered legal assistant is designed to provide information about Moroccan law. It uses advanced AI technology to interpret and respond to legal questions related to Moroccan legislation.

The assistant can help with information about:

  • Civil law and the Moroccan Civil Code
  • Family law and the Moudawana (Family Code)
  • Commercial and business law
  • Property and real estate regulations
  • Labor law and employment rights
  • And many other legal domains specific to Morocco

While this assistant provides helpful information, it should not replace professional legal advice from a qualified Moroccan lawyer.
